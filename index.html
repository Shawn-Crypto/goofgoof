    Your Learning Journey

    Module 1: Foundation - Building Your Investment Mindset

    Duration: 3-4 hours
    Learning Objectives:
    • Understand the difference between investing and trading
    • Learn the core principles of value creation
    • Develop a systematic approach to market analysis
    • Master essential financial terminology
    Key Topics:
    • The Investor's Mindset: Long-term thinking vs. short-term speculation, market psychology, building discipline
    • Financial Literacy Essentials: Reading financial statements, key metrics, industry terminology
    • India's Economic Landscape: Sector overview, growth drivers, regulatory environment
    • Setting Your Framework: Defining your approach, risk assessment, investment criteria

    Module 2: The Analysis Framework - Professional Evaluation Methods

    Duration: 4-5 hours
    Learning Objectives:
    • Master the 4-step company analysis process
    • Understand quality metrics and what they reveal
    • Learn professional valuation techniques
    • Develop your analytical toolkit
    Key Topics:
    • Screening & Filtering: Building effective screens, quality indicators (ROCE, ROE, FCF), red flags
    • Deep Dive Analysis: Business model evaluation, competitive advantage assessment, management quality
    • Financial Statement Mastery: Beyond the numbers, forensic accounting basics, cash flow analysis
    • Valuation Frameworks: Multiple-based valuation, DCF fundamentals, relative valuation techniques

    Module 3: Case Studies - Learning from Real Examples

    Duration: 4-5 hours
    Learning Objectives:
    • Apply frameworks to real companies
    • Understand sector-specific dynamics
    • Learn from both successes and failures
    • Develop pattern recognition skills
    Key Topics:
    • Sector Deep Dives: Telecom transformation (Bharti Airtel), NBFC evolution (Bajaj Finance), consumer goods
    • Turnaround Analysis: Identifying recovery potential, catalyst recognition, risk-reward assessment
    • Crisis Navigation: Market crash responses, opportunity identification, risk management in practice
    • Growth Story Decoding: Sustainable growth identification, scalability assessment, execution track record

    Module 4: Market Dynamics - Understanding the Bigger Picture

    Duration: 3-4 hours
    Learning Objectives:
    • Understand macroeconomic impacts
    • Learn sector rotation strategies
    • Develop market timing insights
    • Master cycle analysis
    Key Topics:
    • Economic Indicators: Interest rate impacts, inflation dynamics, currency considerations
    • Sector Dynamics: Cyclical vs. defensive sectors, rotation patterns, theme identification
    • Market Regimes: Bull vs. bear characteristics, sideways market strategies, volatility navigation
    • Global Influences: FII/DII dynamics, global market correlations, event impact analysis

    Module 5: Portfolio Construction - Building Your Strategy

    Duration: 3-4 hours
    Learning Objectives:
    • Design a balanced portfolio
    • Implement position sizing strategies
    • Develop risk management protocols
    • Create monitoring systems
    Key Topics:
    • Portfolio Architecture: Diversification principles, concentration vs. diversification, asset allocation basics
    • Position Sizing: Conviction-based allocation, risk-based sizing, pyramiding strategies
    • Risk Management: Systematic risk controls, hedging basics, drawdown management
    • Portfolio Maintenance: Rebalancing strategies, performance tracking, tax considerations
