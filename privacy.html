Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 5th July 2025

LFG Ventures LLP ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users ("you" or "your"). This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you access our website and enroll in our course, "Beyond the Deck."

By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

  • Personal Identification Information: Name, email address, and phone number provided during registration.
  • Payment Information: We collect payment details necessary to process your course fee. Please note that payments are processed through our third-party payment gateway, Cashfree, which has its own privacy policy. We do not store your full card details on our servers.
  • Technical and Usage Data: Information about how you access and use our website and course platform, including IP address, browser type, and course progress.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used for the following purposes:

  • To Provide and Manage Services: To facilitate your access to "Beyond the Deck," manage your account, and track your progress.
  • For Communication: To send you essential information regarding your course, updates, and support messages. We may also send you marketing communications, which you can opt-out of at any time.
  • To Process Payments: To process the one-time payment or EMI options for the course fee.
  • To Improve Our Services: To understand how our users interact with our course and website, allowing us to enhance user experience and course content.

3. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. These measures include data encryption, secure server infrastructure, and access controls. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

4. Third-Party Services

We utilize third-party services to deliver our course and process payments:

  • Course Hosting: Our course is hosted on Kajabi. Kajabi has its own privacy and data security policies which govern how your data is handled on their platform.
  • Payment Processing: Payments are handled by Cashfree. When you make a payment, you are subject to Cashfree's terms and privacy policy.

We recommend you review the privacy policies of these third-party services.

5. User Rights Under Indian Law

In accordance with the Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules, 2011, under the Information Technology Act, 2000, you have the right to:

  • Access and review the personal information you have provided.
  • Correct or amend any inaccurate or deficient personal information.
  • Withdraw your consent for the use of your data at any time. Please note that withdrawal of consent may result in the termination of your access to the course.

To exercise these rights, please contact our Grievance Officer.

6. Cookie Policy

Our website uses "cookies" to enhance user experience. Cookies are small files placed on your device that help us analyze web traffic and user activity. You have the option to either accept or refuse these cookies and know when a cookie is being sent to your computer. If you choose to refuse our cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of our service.

7. Data Retention

We will retain your personal information only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy and to comply with our legal obligations.
