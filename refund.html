Refund Policy

48-Hour Money-Back Guarantee

We offer a 48-hour money-back guarantee on all course purchases. If you're not completely satisfied with "The Complete Indian Stock Investor" course, you can request a full refund within 48 hours of your purchase.

How Our Refund Policy Works

At Lotuslion Venture LLP, we stand behind the quality of our educational content. We understand that investing in education is an important decision, which is why we offer a straightforward refund policy.

Eligibility for Refund

To be eligible for a refund, you must meet the following criteria:

  • Request must be made within 48 hours of your purchase
  • Purchase must have been made directly through our website (lotuslion.in)
  • You must provide your order ID or payment receipt

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  • Email us at investoreducation@lotuslion.in
  • Include "Refund Request" in the subject line
  • Provide your full name, email address used for purchase, and order ID
  • Briefly explain the reason for your refund request (optional but helpful)

Processing Time

Once we receive your refund request:

  • We will acknowledge receipt within 24 hours
  • Your refund will be processed within 5-7 business days
  • The refund will be credited to your original payment method
  • You will receive an email confirmation once the refund is processed

Important Notes

  • Course access will be revoked upon refund approval
  • Downloaded materials should be deleted from your devices
  • Refund requests made after 48 hours will not be eligible
  • This policy applies to all course tiers and packages
